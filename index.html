What?

ReStructured Manuscripts (RSM) is a suite of tools that help you produce web-first scientific manuscript that just work.

Why?

Your data, your experiments, your tools, your collaborations are web-first. It’s time for your manuscripts to join the party.

How?

Via a new markup language, an online editor, a specialized viewer, tools for authoring and reviewing... and much more.

The Language

With the RSM markup language, you can focus on telling your story and forget about the headaches.

Abstract

Transform the way you publish.

The RSM markup language is designed with simplicity in mind. The base language contains one special character (the colon), three types of tags (block, inline and meta), and only a handful of syntax rules. Learn more.

The Manuscript

Every manuscript authored with RSM is a fully interactive web document, readable in every screen.

Responsiveness to screen size and user preferences, dark mode, configurable font size and family, accessibility tools such as screen readers. Use all of your favorite tools, outside the box.
