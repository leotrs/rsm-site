Base language

At the core of ReStructured Manuscripts is the RSM markup language. Inspired by popular languages such as Markdown, ReST, and LaTeX, RSM strives for simplicity and power.

Every RSM file starts with the :manuscript: tag and ends with the double colon ::. The double colon acts as the closing tag, and it is referred to as Halmos.

:manuscript:

Hello, RSM!

::
